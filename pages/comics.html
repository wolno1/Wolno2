Synopsis

Meet Milo: the absolute pinnacle of ordinary. Senior year, same old routine, same familiar faces, same predictable days, until the day Inu arrives. He doesn't know her, but suddenly, everyone does and everyone around him starts changing in ways he can't quite explain. Milo finds himself caught between the comfort of the familiar and a feeling he can't quite name, watching his world shift as the ticking clock to prom feels like a countdown to something way bigger than just the end of high school. With graduation breathing down his neck, Milo is staring down the biggest choice of his life: does he finally shoot his shot and risk it all for the most Inu-sual girl he's ever met, or does he let his high school story end in one last, spectacular disaster?
