General Questions

What tools do you use to create your art?

I use a Dell Vostro 14 3000 laptop and a HUION Inspiroy H640P tablet.

For software, I primarily used Paint Tool Sai 2 until October 2025, when I transitioned to Clip Studio Paint.

What is the rating of your art?

While I consider myself a SFW account, my art can sometimes include suggestive themes or strong language. I recommend following me if you're at least 16 years old.

Using and Sharing My Art

Can I repost or use your art?

Absolutely! Feel free to repost, use it as profile pictures, or share it on RP accounts. I only ask that you please credit me where possible.

Can I draw your characters?

Yes, I love receiving fanart and gifts! Please feel free to draw my characters, post it, and tag me or DM me, I'd love to see it!!!

However, if the art is NSFW, I won't be able to repost or interact with it publicly.

Can I sell merchandise with your art?

Please do not use my art for commercial purposes without prior permission. If you're interested in licensing my work for merchandise, please reach out to discuss.

Commissions and Support

Do you offer commissions?

Currently, I'm not offering commissions as I am focusing on my comic project. I may open commissions in the future if needed, so don't lose hope!

How can I support you?

If you enjoy my art, you can support me by following me on social media, sharing my work, or donating through Ko-fi . Every bit of support helps!
